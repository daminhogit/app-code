Hybrid cloud - is when an org keeps its data center and also for emergency connects it to a cloud. In situation where there the data center is down, it connects to a public cloud. Pure cloud - single pathway, pure cloud - not recommended for big organisation A load balancer serves as the single point of contact for clients. The load balancer distributes incoming application traffic across multiple targets, such as EC2 instances, in multiple Availability Zones. This increases the availability of your application. You add one or more listeners to your load balancer.
